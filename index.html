Storm Similie

Clouds gather
like a thousand black kites
knotted and trapped on an endless cord

Lightening casts a luminous glare
and thrusts heartless flames
through the shadows of the night

The wind howls
like a thousand wolves calling
for their moonlight master
as waves of steel
pound like hammers
on my defenceless wooden craft

Sheets of rain pummel my body
and I watch the loose rigging
beat inconsistently
like a dying drummer on a battlefield

Untied sails flap relentlessly
against tired old beams
with sounds like wounded soldiers
screaming in pain

I hold the wheel and start to pray
as the ship pitches and rolls
I am nothing but a little pea
being tossed around in a giant boiling cauldron...
